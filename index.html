About Us

FAST Tutoring is a nonprofit organization based in Laguna Hills, California that provides free tutoring in a wide range of subjects.

Free

All our tutoring is free and there is no hidden payment or requirement to gain access to our services.

Personalized

We will make sure that each student receives help remediating their weaknesses and fortifying their strengths.

All Subject

We teach a majority of the core classes you or your child are taking or will take in the future, plus some extracurricular and elective classes!

Student-driven

FAST was created and is run by Laguna Hills High School students who wish to take an initiative in the global issue of education. We know how challenging school can be and how sometimes extra help is not always affordable, which is why we created FAST.

Our Methodology

Our communication, tutoring tools, and technology:

Zoom

Video Conferencing

Zoom will be our main tutoring platform where we will hold one-on-one sessions or group sessions.


Discord

Communications

Discord is an application that acts as a group chat between the tutors and students. It is open 24/7 for students to ask questions and enter quick calls with tutors.

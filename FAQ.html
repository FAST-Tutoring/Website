Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Learn more about FAST!

How do I schedule a tutoring session?

  • Students will fill out a form with name, subject, date/time of meeting, etc.
  • Once the form is submitted, an email will be automatically sent to the student with the Zoom link. You will also have the option to add the meeting to your calendar.
  • Please check your email in case there are any difficulties/conflicts with your tutoring session. You can prevent conflicts by scheduling at least 2 days in advance.
  • Please join your meeting at your requested date and time.
  • If you have any questions, please contact us at our email: fast.tutoring.ngo@gmail.com.

Who are we?

FAST Tutoring is a nonprofit organization based in Laguna Hills, California that provides free tutoring in a wide range of subjects.

Why free?

The current pandemic has negatively affected the learning of millions of students worldwide. Many students are learning remotely at home and therefore will be unable to create a valuable relationship between them and their teacher. Because of this, students feel more unmotivated and are less successful in achieving course mastery. Our mission is to provide a free service to struggling students and help them during these challenging times.

What makes us qualified to tutor?

Our tutors are high-achievers and have succeeded at multiple AP, IB, and honors-level courses. 

What does FAST stand for/mean?

FAST stands for Free, All-Subject Tutoring. It means that we provide tutoring in almost all high school academic subjects and even some extra curriculars without it costing a penny.

Why us?

Besides the fact we tutor for free, since we are high school students we have the first hand experience of what it is like to be a student in our current generation. This means you can rely on our tips and strategies to succeed in the current school curriculum.
